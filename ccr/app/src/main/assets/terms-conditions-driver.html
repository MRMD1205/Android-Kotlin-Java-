TERMS AND CONDITIONS DRIVER

Dear Driver,

In addition to the Code of Conduct for working on Home to School Transport, the following must be adhered to.

VEHICLE CHECKS – Drivers must make sure they have carried out all relevant vehicle checks prior to starting work. Drivers must also ensure the vehicle you are driving is safe and road legal.

EQUIPMENT – Drivers must make sure all equipment required for your route is present, such as seating/fire extinguisher/1st aid kit. Also, including having your Licences/signs on display.

DOCUMENTS – Drivers are to ensure you are carrying all necessary badges/accident forms. Ensure that you and vehicle remain continuously licensed. As soon as any renewals take place, to send documents in to office within 7 days.

INVOICES – Those staff members who are required to submit invoices/timesheets – please ensure you complete and hand these in on the last working day of each month OR if you are on PAYROLL, by the 20th of each month. Invoices must not contain any mistakes or over charges – this will result in delayed payment and/or penalties.

TRAINING – All Drivers must ensure you attend any training that you are invited to and arrive on time.
