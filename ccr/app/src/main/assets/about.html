ABOUT US

CCR provides Home to School Transport for Children with Special Educational Needs. All of our Drivers and Passenger Assistants hold Enhanced Disclosure & Barring Service checks as well as completing tailored training courses for their respective roles.

Our aim is to ensure your child travels to/from school in a secure and safe environment. Any recommendations you can provide to improve this are always welcomed!
